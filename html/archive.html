2022 | Visual Identity

Ridiculous Car Crashing Newmedia Art Exhibition

This fictional showcase exhibits digital artworks capturing silly but desperate moments on the road. Numerous social media channels are filled with funny memes, some of which even include car accidents. The sudden destruction is captured from the perspective of an observer or driver and shared through Social Media as entertainment. This giddy scene allows you to think about what social media might bring and how it works. Splintered fragments of demolition convey unpredictability, and stretched typefaces symbolize uncontrollable speed—Both characteristics are common to both social media and car crashes.

2022 | UIUX | Branding | Visual Identity | Prototyping

Typewood - Font Search App

Typewood is a font search mobile app service. In contemporary times, the importance of visually arranging letters and text has increased for individuals, especially for designers and also all technology users who need to communicate with digital environments. Typewood offers an enhanced experience for font searching, featuring image searches and tag searches based on font classification. Additionally, It provides a platform for professional typographers to actively communicate and exchange information regarding typography.
